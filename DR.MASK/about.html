ABOUT US

Dr. Mask Co has operations in the UK. Our team are passionate about helping everyone, everywhere get the masks they need to keep them safe and give them peace of mind. We produce 2 ranges of masks, the PRO and BASIC, both are certified under European respirator standard EN149 to FFP2. You can learn more on our certifications page The PRO mask lasts for 3-6 months of daily use (200-300 hours) and has filters that use technology developed by the British Military for chemical, nuclear and biological warfare protection. They filter nearly 100% of viruses, bacteria, gas pollution and particulates. They come in a range of 5 sizes and a range of patterns. Each mask comes with a free nose foam and head strap accessory to help ensure maximum filtration and stop glasses fogging. The BASIC range is tested to filter more than 95% of tiny PM2.5 particulates, which are about 1/50th of the width of a human hair and can go deep into the lungs and potentially into the blood stream. The BASIC masks come in 5 sizes and black or navy colour.
